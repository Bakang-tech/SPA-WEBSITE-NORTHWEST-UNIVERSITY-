 ABOUT US

Mafikeng Spa & Resort offers the latest comprehensive health and beauty treatments and techniques designed to rejuvenate weary bodies and minds, ideal for the traveller in need of relaxation. Mafikeng Spa & Resort experience perfectly complements any number of leisure activities at the hotel – a round of golf;
a peaceful interlude during a conference;
or an indulgent pre-wedding pampering session.

Therapies on offer are carefully chosen to create the perfect environment in
which to combat stress and discomforts caused by urban life.
Mafikeng Spa & Resort offers guests a uniquely comprehensive
treatment of mind, body and spirit to invigorate the senses.

Mafikeng Spa & Resort offers a wide range of reviving facials and massages, body treatments such as exfoliations, body wraps, manicures, pedicures and waxing. Mafikeng Spa & Resort offers world class product ranges including Babor, Mama Mio, TheraVine and Theranaka.

Spa Facilities :

Four treatment rooms including two couples rooms equipped with spa baths
Relaxation area with indoor heated whirlpool
Outdoor area with patio, pool and sunbathing loungers
