Mafikeng Spa & Resort offers the latest comprehensive health and beauty treatments and techniques designed to rejuvenate weary bodies and minds, ideal for the traveller in need of relaxation. Mafikeng Spa & Resort experience perfectly complements any number of leisure activities at the hotel – a round of golf;
a peaceful interlude during a conference;
or an indulgent pre-wedding pampering session.

Therapies on offer are carefully chosen to create the perfect environment in
which to combat stress and discomforts caused by urban life.
